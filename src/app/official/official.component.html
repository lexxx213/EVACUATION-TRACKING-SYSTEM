Terms of Use

Using this system indicates that you have read and accept these terms and agree to follow them.

The Tuguegarao City Evacuation Tracking System provides access to information about evacuation shelters, resident check-ins, and other emergency-related data. This system is intended for use by authorized residents, personnel, and city officials involved in disaster response and management.

Account information used to access the system must be accurate and secure. Usernames and passwords should not be shared with unauthorized individuals.

While every effort is made to keep the system information accurate and up-to-date, the system does not replace official records maintained by the City Disaster Risk Reduction and Management Office.

By using this system, you agree to comply with these terms and use the system responsibly.
